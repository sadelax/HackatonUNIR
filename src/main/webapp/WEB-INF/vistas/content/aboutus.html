Our Impact

Crisis Services

The Trevor Project provides crisis intervention and suicide prevention services to LGBTQ young people in crisis. Our trained counselors are available 24/7 via phone, text, and chat to provide support and help to those who need it.

Learn More

TrevorSpace

TrevorSpace is a social networking site for LGBTQ young people. It provides a safe and supportive online community where young people can connect with others, build friendships, and get advice and support from peers.

Learn More

Education & Advocacy

The Trevor Project provides education and advocacy to promote LGBTQ acceptance and support. Our programs include training for educators and mental health professionals, as well as advocacy efforts to change policies and laws that affect LGBTQ young people.

Learn More

Research & Training

The Trevor Project conducts research to better understand the needs of LGBTQ young people and improve our services. We also provide training to other organizations and individuals to help them better serve the LGBTQ community.
